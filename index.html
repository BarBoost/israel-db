üõà How to use the database

This database is based on the Airtable platform, which provides advanced filtering, grouping, and sorting capabilities.
These tools are super powerful and can save you a lot of time searching.

For simple use, you can click on any of the files in the "Attachments" column and then the "Download" button on the bottom right.


Features

  • Filter: Filter out records from a view according to the conditions you set. A condition is a rule you set that records must follow to be visible. Click here to learn more.
  • Group: The grouped records feature allows you to show your records grouped together based on one (or more) field of your choosing. Click here to learn more.
  • Sort: The sorting records feature allows you to sort your records so that they appear in a particular order according to the values in specific fields. Click here to learn more.
  • Search: Click on the magnifier icon on the top right to open the search field of the database.
  • Row height: There are four different row height options. To change the row height, click the row height switcher in the view bar, then select the desired height.
  • Hide fields: Allow you to hide columns you don't need from the view.
  • View larger version: Click on the bottom right button to open a full-screen version in a new tab. 


Filtering and grouping options

  • Format: Choose between Text, Image, Video, GIF, or Audio content.
  • Orientation: Find content for specific use cases, e.g., portrait files for Story, Reel, or TikTok videos.
  • Topics: The main categorization filters that allow you to find content on different topics and issues.
  • Language: Filter content for specific language.
  • Graphic: Graphic and violent content.
